About Us

Welcome, Homey is an international flatshare platform!

Skyrocket rise of rental in cities, teenagers are unable to afford rental alone. We believe flat sharing with roommate would be the solution.

Compared to traditional rental websites, we put emphasis on interaction between landlord and tenant. They could post their respective ad,

so that they could match up rental criteria such as price, area, move-in date, giving a smooth and quick rental process.
